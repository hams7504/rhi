About Tele-Friend

Pen-pal to the elderly program: This program focuses on combating issues of loneliness and social isolation in rural elderly populations. By partnering college students with a “pen-pal” elderly partner, students can engage in virtual discussions and activities to help provide companionship to those who need it most.
